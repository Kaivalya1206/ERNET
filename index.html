Network
ERNET India is running MPLS enabled dual stack (IPv4 and IPv6) backbone. The ERNET backbone interconnects ERNET Point of Presence (PoP) spread throughout the country.
Services
In tune with the mandate ERNET is providing four types of services, namely, Access Services, Application Services, Hosting Services and Operations Support Services
Research&Development
ERNET India is actively involved in research and development in the field of computer networking. Current areas of interest include high speed networking.
Training
The functions of ERNET India are characterized by advanced technologies of diverse nature in the field of high-end & next generation networks, LAN/WAN, Campus Networks, ICT.
Projects
ERNET has conceptualized, developed and implemented large number of projects on turn-key basis for various Educational Institutes and Research Organization.
